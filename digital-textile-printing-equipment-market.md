<p><strong>The Digital Textile Printing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct to Fabric</li><li>Direct to Garment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital textile printing equipment includes two primary market types: Direct to Fabric (DTF) and Direct to Garment (DTG). Direct to Fabric printing applies ink directly onto fabrics before they are cut and sewn, making it ideal for large rolls of textiles used in fashion and home décor. In contrast, Direct to Garment printing focuses on printing directly onto finished apparel, allowing for detailed designs and customization in smaller quantities, making it popular for on-demand and personalized fashion items.</p></p>
