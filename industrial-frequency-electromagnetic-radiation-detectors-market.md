<p><p>Industrial frequency electromagnetic radiation detectors are categorized based on the frequency ranges they monitor. Low frequency detectors capture signals at frequencies typically below 300 kHz, often used in power generation and transmission monitoring. High frequency detectors operate between 300 kHz and 3 GHz, suitable for telecommunications and broadcasting applications. Ultra high frequency detectors operate above 3 GHz, commonly utilized in radar systems and advanced communication technologies. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing safety and efficiency in electromagnetic field management.</p></p>
