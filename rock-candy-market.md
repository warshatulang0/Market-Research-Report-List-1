<p><p>The rock candy market comprises primarily two types: Monocrystalline (or Monocrystal) rock sugar and Natural rock sugar. Monocrystalline rock sugar is characterized by its uniform crystal structure, providing a clean and refined appearance, often used in desserts and beverages. Natural rock sugar, on the other hand, is less processed, retaining some minerals and offering a more rustic look and flavor. Both types cater to various consumer preferences, emphasizing purity, taste, and culinary versatility in sweetening applications.</p></p>
