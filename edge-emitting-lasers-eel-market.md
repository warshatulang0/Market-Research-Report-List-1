<p><p>Edge Emitting Lasers (EEL) are semiconductor lasers where light is emitted from the edge of the diode. The market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Distributed Feedback Laser (DFB)**: Uses a periodic structure for wavelength stabilization, ideal for telecommunications.  </p><p>2. **Distributed Bragg Reflector Laser (DBR)**: Combines feedback and gain to enhance performance and stability.  </p><p>3. **Fabry-Perot Laser**: Features a simple cavity design for moderate efficiency and cost-effective production.  </p><p>4. **Broad Area Laser Diodes**: Offer high output power with a larger active area, suitable for industrial applications.</p></p>
