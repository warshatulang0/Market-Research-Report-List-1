<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li><li>Type III</li><li>Type IV</li><li>Type V</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental plaster is categorized into five types based on specific applications and properties. Type I is used for impressions in prosthodontics. Type II is suitable for making models and casts. Type III, known as stone, offers increased strength and is ideal for detailed casts. Type IV is a high-strength stone used for working models, providing superior accuracy. Type V has the highest expansion and strength, making it perfect for dental restorations that require precise fit and durability in challenging environments.</p></p>
