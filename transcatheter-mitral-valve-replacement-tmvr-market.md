<p><p>The Transcatheter Mitral Valve Replacement (TMVR) market focuses on treating mitral valve disorders, particularly Functional Mitral Regurgitation (FMR) and Degenerative Mitral Regurgitation (DMR). FMR arises from heart muscle dysfunction, often related to heart failure, impacting flow dynamics. DMR, on the other hand, is caused by structural valve problems like prolapse or calcification. TMVR offers a less invasive alternative for patients with significant regurgitation, improving clinical outcomes and quality of life while addressing the unique challenges posed by each condition.</p></p>
