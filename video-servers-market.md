<p><p>The video servers market is categorized into M-JPEG, MPEG-4, and H.264 technologies, each catering to different performance needs. M-JPEG compresses video frames individually, offering quality but at higher bandwidth usage, ideal for surveillance. MPEG-4 provides better compression and video quality, making it suitable for streaming and media applications. H.264, known for its efficient compression, balances video quality and file size, making it widely adopted in broadcasting and online streaming. Each technology serves specific market demands based on quality and efficiency.</p></p>
