<p><p>The IVF incubators market encompasses two primary types: benchtop/tabletop incubators and floor-based incubators. Benchtop/tabletop incubators are compact units designed for smaller laboratories, making them ideal for limited spaces and facilitating easy handling and accessibility. In contrast, floor-based incubators are larger units suitable for high-volume operations, offering greater capacity for multiple cultures. Both types are essential in maintaining optimal environmental conditions for embryo development, ensuring precise temperature, humidity, and gas levels crucial for successful IVF procedures.</p></p>
