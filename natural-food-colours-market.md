<p><p>The natural food colors market consists of various types, each derived from natural sources. Carotenoids provide yellow and orange hues, primarily from carrots and tomatoes. Curcumin, extracted from turmeric, offers a vibrant yellow color. Anthocyanins, found in berries, deliver red, purple, and blue shades. Paprika extract adds red color from capsicum, while spirulina extract contributes blue-green tones from algae. Chlorophyll imparts green colors from plants, and carmine, sourced from cochineal insects, yields a deep red shade. These natural colorants are increasingly favored for their safety and health benefits.</p></p>
