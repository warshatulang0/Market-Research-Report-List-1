<p><p>The air-water heat pump market can be categorized into three main types: Low Temperature Type, High Temperature Type, and Hybrid Type. Low Temperature Type heat pumps operate efficiently in milder climates, providing heating at lower temperatures, ideal for underfloor heating systems. High Temperature Type heat pumps cater to colder environments, delivering higher temperature outputs suitable for traditional radiators. Hybrid Type systems combine the benefits of both, utilizing both air and water sources to optimize efficiency and performance across varying temperature conditions.</p></p>
