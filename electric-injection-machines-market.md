<p><strong>The Electric Injection Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Injection Machines</li><li>Horizontal Injection Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric injection machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their configuration and operational mechanics. Vertical injection machines utilize a vertical clamping unit, offering a compact design ideal for small spaces and applications like insert molding. In contrast, horizontal injection machines feature a horizontal clamping unit, which allows for larger mold sizes and higher production rates, making them favorable for extensive production runs. Each type serves specific industry needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in plastic molding processes.</p></p>
