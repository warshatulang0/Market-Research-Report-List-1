<p><strong>The Column-Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brushed Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Column-Electric Power Steering (C-EPS) market comprises two main types: Brushed Motor and Brushless Motor systems. Brushed Motor systems utilize traditional commutation with carbon brushes, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but may require more maintenance. In contrast, Brushless Motor systems employ electronic commutation, resulting in higher efficiency, reduced noise, and longer life spans. Brushless systems are increasingly favored for modern vehicles due to their superior performance and reliability, driving advancements in steering technology and enhancing overall vehicle dynamics.</p></p>
