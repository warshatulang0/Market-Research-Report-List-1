<p><p>The epithelial stem cells market encompasses various specialized cells with distinct functions. Absorptive cells are crucial for nutrient uptake in the intestine, enhancing digestive efficiency. Goblet cells produce mucus, protecting and lubricating the intestinal lining. Paneth cells secrete antimicrobial molecules, playing a key role in gut immunity. Oligodendrocytes are responsible for myelinating neurons in the central nervous system, ensuring efficient signal transmission. Together, these cell types contribute to tissue maintenance and immune responses, making their market vital for regenerative medicine and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
