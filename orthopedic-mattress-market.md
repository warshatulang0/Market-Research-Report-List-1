<p><p>The orthopedic mattress market is divided into two main types: spring orthopedic mattresses and non-spring orthopedic mattresses. Spring orthopedic mattresses utilize innerspring coils to provide support and responsiveness, catering to those who prefer a traditional feel. In contrast, non-spring orthopedic mattresses, which include memory foam, latex, and air mattresses, offer enhanced pressure relief and contouring support, making them suitable for individuals with specific orthopedic needs. Both types aim to improve sleep quality and alleviate pain, promoting better spinal alignment.</p></p>
