<p><p>The Thermal Energy Storage market comprises three main types: Sensible Heat Storage, Latent Heat Storage, and Thermochemical Storage. Sensible Heat Storage involves storing thermal energy by raising the temperature of a material, typically water or molten salts. Latent Heat Storage utilizes phase change materials that absorb or release energy during phase transitions, enhancing efficiency. Thermochemical Storage relies on chemical reactions to store and release thermal energy, offering high energy density. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing energy management and sustainability.</p></p>
