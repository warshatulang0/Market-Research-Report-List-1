<p><p>The Industrial UV Inkjet Ink market is categorized into two main types based on printing technology: multi-pass and single-pass printers. Inkjet ink for multi-pass printers is designed for applications requiring high detail and quality, as the print head makes multiple passes over the substrate. In contrast, inkjet ink for single-pass printers enables faster production speeds, as the print head moves in a single pass, making it ideal for high-volume printing tasks. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
