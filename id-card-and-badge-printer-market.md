<p><strong>The ID Card and Badge Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Sub Printers</li><li>Retransfer Printers</li><li>Inkjet Printers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ID card and badge printer market primarily consists of three types: dye sublimation (dye sub) printers, retransfer printers, and inkjet printers. Dye sub printers use heat to transfer dye onto the card for vibrant, durable prints. Retransfer printers print on a film that is then fused to the card, offering high-quality images, especially on uneven surfaces. Inkjet printers, while less common for ID cards, utilize liquid ink and are suitable for lower volume printing with intricate designs but may lack durability compared to the other types.</p></p>
