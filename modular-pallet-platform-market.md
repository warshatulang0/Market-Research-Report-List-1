Roller platforms facilitate easy transportation of goods, with driven rollers powered by motors or gravity-activated rollers for cost-effective solutions. Plastic platforms are lightweight and resistant to corrosion, making them suitable for diverse environments. Slat type platforms offer versatility and support for different packaging needs, enhancing operational flexibility.</p></p>
