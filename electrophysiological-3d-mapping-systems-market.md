<p><p>Electrophysiological 3D mapping systems are crucial in diagnosing and treating cardiac arrhythmias. The market is segmented into non-contact and contact mapping systems. Non-contact mapping systems utilize electromagnetic fields to create detailed cardiac maps without direct contact, allowing for rapid collection of data across the heart's surface. In contrast, contact mapping systems involve direct contact with the heart tissue, enabling precise voltage and activation mapping. Both types enhance procedural efficacy and patient outcomes in electrophysiological interventions.</p></p>
