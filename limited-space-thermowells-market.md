<p><p>The limited space thermowell market encompasses three main types: limited space straight thermowells, limited space stepped thermowells, and limited space tapered thermowells. Limited space straight thermowells are designed for applications with restricted installation areas, providing a direct path for temperature measurement. Limited space stepped thermowells feature a varying diameter to facilitate compatibility with different fittings while maintaining structural integrity. Limited space tapered thermowells gradually narrow, ideal for tight spaces, ensuring accurate temperature readings without compromising performance or reliability.</p></p>
