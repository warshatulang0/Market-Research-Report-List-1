<p><strong>The Continuous Flow Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Segmented Flow Analyzer</li><li>Injected Flow Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Continuous Flow Analyzer market comprises two main types: Segmented Flow Analyzers and Injected Flow Analyzers. Segmented Flow Analyzers operate by introducing discrete segments of sample and reagents into a flow path, allowing for precise control and analysis of samples, ideal for varied chemical tests. Injected Flow Analyzers, on the other hand, utilize a single continuous stream where samples are injected, enabling rapid analysis without the need for segmentation. Both types cater to diverse analytical requirements in laboratories and industrial applications.</p></p>
