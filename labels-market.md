<p><p>The labels market comprises various types catering to different applications. Pressure-sensitive labels adhere without the need for heat or water, making them versatile for various products. Glue-applied labels involve an adhesive application, often providing a strong bond and aesthetic appeal. Sleeve labels wrap around products, offering 360-degree branding and are commonly used in beverage packaging. In-mold labels are integrated during the molding process, creating durable labels that withstand rigorous conditions, ideal for containers and specific consumer goods.</p></p>
