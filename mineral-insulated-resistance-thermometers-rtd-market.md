<p><p>The Mineral Insulated Resistance Thermometers (RTD) market is categorized into two primary types: simplex conductor and duplex conductor. Simplex conductors consist of a single sensing element, providing basic temperature measurement for a wide range of applications. In contrast, duplex conductors feature two sensing elements, allowing for redundancy, improved accuracy, and the ability to compensate for potential failures. This dual capability makes duplex RTDs ideal for critical applications where precise temperature readings are essential for safety and efficiency.</p></p>
