<p><strong>The Off-Road Vehicle HID Lamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Built-In Ballast</li><li>External Ballast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Off-road vehicle HID lamps are categorized based on their ballast configuration: built-in ballast and external ballast. Built-in ballast systems have the ballast integrated within the lamp housing, providing a compact design and simplifying installation. Conversely, external ballast systems feature the ballast as a separate unit, allowing for easier maintenance and replacement but requiring more installation space. Both types offer enhanced illumination and durability for off-road conditions, catering to diverse user preferences in vehicle setups and lighting performance.</p></p>
