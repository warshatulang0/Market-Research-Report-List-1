<p><p>Formulation additives enhance product performance across various industries, including paints, coatings, and personal care. Defoamers reduce foam formation, improving application efficiency and surface quality. Rheology modifiers adjust the flow and viscosity of formulations, ensuring optimal application and stability. Dispersing agents facilitate the uniform distribution of pigments and solids, preventing clumping and ensuring consistent color and texture. Together, these additives contribute to the overall quality, usability, and aesthetics of formulated products, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
