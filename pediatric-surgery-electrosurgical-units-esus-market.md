<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pediatric surgery electrosurgical units (ESUs) market includes various types designed for surgical applications in children. Monopole electrosurgical units use a single active electrode to cut or coagulate tissue, while bipolar electrosurgical units utilize two prongs to apply current directly across tissue, minimizing thermal spread. Vessel sealing units focus on securing blood vessels through high-frequency energy, improving hemostasis. These specialized devices enhance surgical precision, reduce complications, and optimize outcomes in pediatric surgical procedures.</p></p>
