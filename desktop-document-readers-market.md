<p><p>Desktop document readers encompass various technologies designed for scanning and interpreting documents. MSI (Magnetic Stripe Interface) readers process data from magnetic stripes, commonly found on ID cards and credit cards. RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) readers utilize electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects, enhancing inventory management. OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology converts different types of documents, such as scanned paper documents or images, into editable and searchable data. Other types may include barcode scanners or specialized document management systems, catering to diverse business needs.</p></p>
