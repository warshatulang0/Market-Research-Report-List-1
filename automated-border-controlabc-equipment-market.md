<p><p>Automated Border Control (ABC) equipment encompasses two main market types: ABC e-gates and ABC kiosks. ABC e-gates are automated entry/exit points that use biometric technology for seamless traveler verification, enhancing security and efficiency at border crossings. In contrast, ABC kiosks are self-service stations where travelers input their information and biometric data, streamlining the check-in process before proceeding through border control. Both solutions aim to expedite passenger processing while maintaining stringent security measures at borders.</p></p>
