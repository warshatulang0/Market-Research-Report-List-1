<p><p>The dialysis equipment and services market consists of two primary types: hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis products and supplies. Hemodialysis involves the use of machines and kits to filter blood externally, including dialyzers, bloodlines, and dialysis machines. Peritoneal dialysis, on the other hand, utilizes the patient's peritoneal membrane for filtration, requiring catheter systems and dialysis solutions. Both segments cater to patients with chronic kidney failure, providing essential products and services for effective renal replacement therapy.</p></p>
