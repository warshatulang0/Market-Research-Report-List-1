Liquid sachet packing machines cater to beverages, sauces, and oils, ensuring secure packaging to prevent leakage. Powder sachet packaging machines handle dry substances like spices, coffee, or detergents, providing accurate dosing and airtight sealing. Each type serves distinct product needs while enhancing shelf life and convenience.</p></p>
