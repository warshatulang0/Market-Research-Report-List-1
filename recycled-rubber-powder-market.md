<p><ul><li>Dry Processing</li><li>Wet Processing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The recycled rubber powder market is divided into two primary processing types: dry processing and wet processing. </p><p>Dry processing involves mechanically grinding scrap rubber into fine powder without additional water, leading to a cost-effective and energy-efficient method. </p><p>Wet processing, on the other hand, utilizes water during the grinding process, which can enhance the quality and consistency of the powder but often requires more investment in equipment. Both methods cater to varying applications in industries like automotive, construction, and sports.</p></p>
