<p><p>The distilled fatty acid market comprises three main types: palm kernel, coconut, and palm oil. Palm kernel fatty acids are derived from the extraction of oil from palm kernels and are valued for their high lauric acid content, making them suitable for cosmetics and personal care products. Coconut fatty acids are extracted from the meat of coconuts, prized for their emollient properties. Palm oil fatty acids, sourced from the pulp of palm fruit, are widely used in food processing and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct markets, leveraging their unique properties.</p></p>
