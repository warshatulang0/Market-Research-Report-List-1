<p><p>Composite pipes in construction are categorized mainly into clad pipes and non-metal composite pipes. Clad pipes combine metal and non-metal materials, offering enhanced corrosion resistance and strength, often used in demanding environments. Non-metal composite pipes, on the other hand, are made entirely from non-metal materials, such as fiber-reinforced polymers, providing lightweight, flexible, and corrosion-resistant solutions. Both types are essential for modern construction, catering to diverse applications while improving durability and reducing maintenance needs.</p></p>
