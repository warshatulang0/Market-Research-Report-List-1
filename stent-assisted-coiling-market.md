<p><strong>The Stent Assisted Coiling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-Expandable Stents</li><li>Liquid Embolic Devices</li><li>Flow-diversion Devices</li><li>Embolic Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stent Assisted Coiling market comprises several key types. Self-expandable stents are used to support blood vessels during aneurysm treatment. Liquid embolic devices, such as n-butyl cyanoacrylate, offer a minimally invasive option to occlude blood flow. Flow-diversion devices redirect blood flow away from an aneurysm, promoting healing. Embolic coils are introduced into the aneurysm to induce clotting and reduce risk. Each of these devices plays a crucial role in effectively treating vascular conditions, ensuring patient safety and improving outcomes.</p></p>
