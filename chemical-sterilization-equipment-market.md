Hydrogen peroxide sterilization employs vaporized hydrogen peroxide, providing rapid and residue-free results. Ozone-based sterilization uses ozone gas for its strong oxidative properties. Formaldehyde sterilization involves fumigation or vaporization, suitable for various settings.
