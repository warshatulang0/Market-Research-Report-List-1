<p><strong>The Semiconductor Scroll Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Vortex</li><li>Moving Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor scroll pump market is divided into two primary types: static vortex and moving vortex. Static vortex pumps utilize a fixed geometry to create a stable vortex flow, offering efficient vacuum generation with minimal moving parts, making them reliable for semiconductor processes. In contrast, moving vortex pumps employ rotating elements to enhance performance and adaptability, enabling them to handle varying flow rates and pressures. Both types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing for maintaining clean environments and precise vacuum conditions.</p></p>
